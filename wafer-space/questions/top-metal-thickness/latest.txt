From https://github.com/RTimothyEdwards/open_pdks/blob/master/gf180mcu/NOTES:
"February 14, 2023:  Added PDK variant gf180mcuD, which is like gf180mcuC except has a top metal thickness of 1.1um instead of 0.9um.
