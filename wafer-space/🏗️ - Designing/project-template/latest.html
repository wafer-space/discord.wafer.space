mole99 11/03/2025 20:35
This is the ‚ú® magic ‚ú® line for the gf180mcu_fd_ip_sram__sram512x8m8wm1 SRAM: add_pdn_stripe -grid macro -layer Metal4 -width 2.36 -offset 1.18 -spacing 0.28 -pitch 426.86 -starts_with GROUND -number_of_straps 2 It adds a power/ground pair on each side of the SRAM macro, directly above the pins. Since this uses the local coordinate system of the macro, you can move the macro around as you wish, the PDN straps will follow. As the PDN straps go all the way across the macro, connecting them to the PDN above is very easy.
