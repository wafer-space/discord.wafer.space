[11/24/2025 23:03] anfroholic
There are something like 10 ground pins on the standard cob, it isn't necessary to have the routing down the cob stay the same as the default. 
The part (for now) that seems most important is to keep the wirebonding pads (qty74) and the 74 pads in the default padring, then also the actual PCB to keep the 14x16mm size and the 70pin mezzanine. 
@xianglin_pu and the Mosbius team have created their own routing to gain some extra pins by not having so many vss/vcc etc. 
https://discord.com/channels/1361349522684510449/1429068742108909638/1429520076536811622
