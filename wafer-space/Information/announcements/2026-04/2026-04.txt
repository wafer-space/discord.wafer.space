[04/26/2026 01:29] mithro_
@everyone - Just another quick update!

The first round of chip on board packages went out yesterday (only 1x1 parts using the standard cob PCB layout). These packages have ~25 of people's die mounted on the PCBs. This was our first attempt at doing this workflow and here are a bunch of things we are working to improve before we do bigger runs.

**Could everyone confirm when they have received their packages and try to test them as soon as possible?** It would be good to first confirm you got the expected die - I forgot to do a visual inspection of that before shipping.

From our tests with the Tiny Tapeout die, we had ~20% of the packaged die fail testing, ***likely*** to bond wire lifting off pads.  We have a bunch of pending changes which we hopefully will reduce that to something more like 5% or 1%. 

This version of the PCB also has a small error which caused the `PWR_AUX_1` trace not to be connected to the header.

We will probably be working on the non-1x1 die and custom PCB layout stuff this week.
